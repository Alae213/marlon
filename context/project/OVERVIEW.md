> Generated by setup wizard. Updated from PRD.md analysis.

## What It Is

**Marlon** is a multi-tenant SaaS platform that enables Algerian entrepreneurs to create and manage Cash-on-Delivery (COD) online stores with zero technical knowledge.

Think of it as Shopify, but simplified for the Algerian market — no domain setup, no payment processing complexity, just a store that works with local delivery companies.

## The Problem

Algerian small businesses and solo sellers face significant barriers when trying to sell online:

1. **Complexity** — Existing platforms (Shopify, WooCommerce) are overkill and require technical skills
2. **Payment Friction** — International payment gateways don't work well with DZD
3. **Missing COD Workflows** — No built-in order management for COD specifically
4. **Fragmented Tooling** — Need separate tools for store, orders, delivery, and payments

Most merchants resort to WhatsApp or Facebook orders — no proper catalog, no order tracking, manual everything.

## The Solution

Marlon provides a complete, zero-setup solution:
- Create a store in minutes (no technical skills needed)
- COD-first workflow built-in (order management, call logging, status tracking)
- Integrated with local delivery companies (ZR Express, Yalidine)
- Simple billing: 50 free orders, then 2000 DZD/month

## Differentiator

- **COD-Native**: Built specifically for cash-on-delivery workflows (call logging, status machine, customer masking)
- **Zero Technical Skills**: Create a store in minutes, add products, start selling — no setup required
- **Algeria-Focused**: Wilaya/commune dropdowns, Algerian phone validation, local delivery integrations, DZD pricing
- **Pay-When-You-Succeed**: 50 free orders, then 2000 DZD/month — no credit card required, manual renewal
